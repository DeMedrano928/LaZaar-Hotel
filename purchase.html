Items necessary for the care of a Capybara

  • Pools/Ponds to help them stay cool when they're hot.
  • Variety of Vegetables to encourage a healthy and diverse diet.
  • Straw/Hay bedding or Fleece liners to give them a spot to rest.
  • Chew toys & mental stimulating toys to prevent boredom and maintain mental stimulation.
  • Spacious enclosure with a body of water to prevent any injuries, or mental stress and a comfortable space for the capybara to rest.
